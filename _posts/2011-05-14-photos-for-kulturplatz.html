After a request from a guy on a Radiohead forum, last week I took lots of pictures of the King of Limbs newspaper album for a short report he was producing for Swiss TV. The brief was to take photos of the individual elements (vinyl, CD, artwork and newspaper) from above. Nothing fancy... i.e. not slanted; no angles, I believe were his words.

Anyway, it has now been on TV and, if you want, you can watch it here. The photos are mostly near the start and near the end. I had to take them outside to take advantage of the evening light, so you'll notice our paving stones in some of the shots. I kind of assumed he'd crop those out, but what the hell.
