--- layout: post title: '78/365: Sick!' date: 2011-03-29 ---

Day 78 was a success, I'd say, and produced one of my favourites so far. This was taken just a short walk from my house at Reddicth Skate & BMX park. There were loads of people there, and I got so many almost great photos of them mid-jump (including a few of them upside down, mid-flip), but they were all moving so quickly that they weren't completely in frame.

Some of the guys wanted to see what pictures I took and they reliably informed me that they're sick photos. That means they liked them.

I'm sure I'll be heading back there a couple more times before the project is up.
