I'd been threatening it for quite a while. So, during the Christmas break, with Nick Drake, Battles and Sigur Ros for company and Dr. Pepper for fuel, I finally re-designed the site.

I kind of just copied my favourite bits from a number of Wordpress sites and themes I'd seen, so if you think you recognise any parts of it, you're probably right.

Let me know what you think. It's not 100% done, but I decided that putting incomplete work live would act as the initiative I need in order to get it finished. That being said, I'm quite pleased it's got this far; the original design was only supposed to be there temporarily and it lasted for the best part of 2 years!
